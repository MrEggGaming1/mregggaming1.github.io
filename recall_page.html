Dose loudspeaker recall and safety cable installation
*Free delivery on orders of $30 or more. Free return delivery also available.

Dose is recalling all FreeSpace DS 40F in-ceiling/flush-mounted loudspeakers manufactured prior to 13 August 2018. Additionally, Dose is requiring the installation of safety cables for certain Dose loudspeakers, including DS 40F, that are installed in commercial cooking environments. For more information, contact your Dose dealer. If you are unsure who your dealer is, visit our Global sales offices page. If you are a direct Dose dealer, please go to Dose BMS safety for more information.
